## 2. The Client Website Visibility Report

**What It Is**

A single, comprehensive report that covers all 11 stages of the AI Visibility Lifecycle on your website.

This report measures both what AI systems are doing with your website and what human outcomes are resulting from AI visibility. It replaces fragmented metrics from legacy tools with a unified view of where your website stands in AI-mediated discovery.

**What It Delivers**

The Client Website Visibility Report measures status across all 11 stages of the AI Visibility Lifecycle:

| Stage | What AI Does | What The Report Measures |
|-------|-------------|------------------------|
| 1. AI Crawling | Discovers and fetches pages | Crawler access status |
| 2. AI Ingestion | Parses and creates semantic embeddings | Content processing status |
| 3. AI Classification | Determines website purpose | Purpose clarity |
| 4. AI Harmony Checks | Evaluates internal consistency | Structural coherence |
| 5. AI Cross-Correlation | Verifies alignment with authoritative sources | External alignment |
| 6. AI Trust Building | Accumulates reliability evidence | Trust signal strength |
| 7. AI Trust Acceptance | Marks domain as eligible for answers | Trust threshold status |
| 8. Candidate Surfacing | Assesses competitive readiness | Competitive positioning |
| 9. Early Human Testing | Tests with real users | User response signals |
| 10. Baseline Ranking | First stable placement | Initial traffic baseline |
| 11. Growth Visibility | Expands visibility at scale | Traffic acceleration |

The report answers four questions through this evidence: Where are we? What changed? What does it mean? What should we do?

The first three are answered through evidence. The fourth is answered through professional judgment — analysis that turns data into direction.

**The Evidence Standard**

Every finding in this report meets a strict admissible evidence standard:

**Observable.** The signal is directly observable — no inferences, no assumptions.

**Verifiable.** The finding can be verified — if challenged, the evidence can be shown.

**Repeatable.** The observation can be repeated — not a one-time anomaly.

This standard protects both agencies and business owners. What cannot be evidenced is not claimed.

## 3. What Your Agency Gets

**Stop the False Underperformance Narrative**

This is already happening inside boardrooms: 'SEO used to work — now it's broken.' 'The agency isn't delivering like before.' 'We're losing ground to competitors.'

In reality, competitors may be losing too, discovery may be shifting to AI, and authority may be rising unseen. But tool-based reporting cannot show that.

Website Visibility Reporting gives you evidence to counter false narratives. When clients question performance based on declining traffic or rankings, you show them what's actually happening — where their website sits in the AI visibility lifecycle, whether AI systems are accessing and trusting it, and whether human outcomes are emerging.

You stop defending metrics that no longer matter. You start reporting on metrics that do.

**Answer the Questions CMOs Are Asking**

CMOs are beginning to ask questions that traditional tools cannot answer:

- What percentage of your recommended activities target AI visibility vs. traditional rankings?
- How do you measure authority beyond Domain Authority / Domain Rating?
- Can you show me where our content is being cited by AI systems?
- What's your transition plan as traditional SEO metrics decline?
- Which of your tools will still be relevant in 24 months?

If you cannot answer these questions, your clients will find someone who can.

Website Visibility Reporting equips you with answers. You can explain what AI visibility means, show where the client's website stands, and demonstrate progress through evidence — not promises.

**Defensible Reporting**

Every finding in the Client Website Visibility Report traces to observable evidence. No black boxes. No proprietary scores you cannot explain.

When a board member challenges a finding, you show them the source. When a CMO asks how you know, you explain. When a CEO demands accountability, you deliver.

This is reporting designed for scrutiny — because that's where agencies are increasingly operating.

**Protect Client Budgets**

Budgets continue flowing into keyword expansion, rank tracking, SERP feature optimisation, backlink acquisition for authority metrics, and CTR experiments. Many of these activities have no transformation path — they simply cease to matter.

That means money isn't just low-ROI; it's funding work with zero future carryover value. Sunk cost with opportunity loss layered on top.

Website Visibility Reporting helps you redirect client investment toward activities that build AI visibility — work that compounds over time rather than depreciates.

**Retain High-Value Clients**

The clients who matter most — authority-driven sectors, long-cycle B2B, content-heavy brands — are the ones most likely to seek agencies that understand AI visibility. They are also the most valuable clients.

Agencies that cannot measure AI visibility will lose these clients to those who can. Website Visibility Reporting positions you as the agency that understands where discovery is going — not the one still optimising for rankings.

**Lead the Transition**

Every quarter spent optimising for traditional SEO success delays AI visibility architecture. AI trust compounds slowly but powerfully. Clients who delay adaptation by 12–18 months don't just lag — they fall into structural catch-up mode. That gap is hard to close later.

Agencies that adapt will capture the clients who understand what's happening. Agencies that don't will become the market's casualties — blamed for performance declines they didn't cause, losing clients to competitors who speak the new language, and defending tools that no longer defend them.

Website Visibility Reporting is how you lead, not follow.

Your CMO reports one story. AI reality tells another. Your board sees declining charts. Budgets get cut. Teams argue about 'underperformance' that may not be real.

The Client Website Visibility Report gives you the evidence you need to make correct decisions — not reactive ones based on metrics that no longer reflect how discovery works.

The Client Website Visibility Report helps you understand where your investment is going and whether it's building AI visibility that compounds over time — or funding work with zero future carryover value.

**Board-Ready Evidence**

You need reports designed for decision-makers, not technicians. You need evidence you can present to your board. Findings your CEO can understand. Evidence that's defensible under scrutiny.

The Client Website Visibility Report is built for this purpose. Every finding traces to observable evidence. No black boxes. No proprietary scores that can't be explained. When challenged, the evidence can be shown.

**Clear Action, Not Just Data**

Traditional reporting leaves you with data but no direction. You know traffic moved but not why. You know rankings changed but not what it means. You see numbers but not next steps.

The Client Website Visibility Report includes Account Manager analysis and recommendations. Not just what happened — what it means and what to do about it. Prioritised next steps. Resource considerations. Expected outcomes.

Repackaged SEO reporting only shows the end result — citation. It's like a sales report that shows revenue but hides lead generation, qualification, proposals, and close rates. You know you made money, but you have no idea why, and no ability to diagnose or improve performance.

The Client Website Visibility Report shows status across the entire pipeline — not just outcomes. You see where your website is succeeding, where it's failing, and what's being done about it.

That's not hope. That's accountability.

## 5. The AIVA Framework

For those who want to understand the underlying structure, this section outlines the AIVA framework.

**The 11-Stage AI Visibility Lifecycle**

The AIVA framework maps website visibility across 11 stages — from initial AI discovery through to sustained human traffic. The lifecycle tracks how AI systems discover, ingest, classify, trust, and ultimately cite website content, and how that trust translates into human engagement.

The stages are documented in AIVA methodology materials and disclosed under engagement.

**What The Report Does Not Claim**

This report focuses on what can be observed and verified through the AIVA methodology. It does not include:

- Rankings inside AI models
- Training inclusion assertions
- Token usage or context window allocation
- Model preference claims
- Traffic value or revenue attribution
- Future ranking predictions
- Guaranteed visibility outcomes

Other methodologies may approach these differently. This report is bounded by the admissible evidence standard — observable, verifiable, repeatable.

Competitor visibility comparison methodologies are in development as AI measurement systems mature.

**The Account Manager Role**

The Client Website Visibility Report is not a dashboard. It is a professional deliverable that requires interpretation.

The Account Manager:

- Reads and understands the full Website Visibility evidence base
- Synthesises technical evidence into comprehensible findings
- Interprets what the data means for this specific situation
- Provides honest assessment of current status
- Makes specific recommendations for improvement
- Empowers informed decision-making

**The Account Manager observes, interprets, analyses, recommends, and empowers. The client decides and acts.**
